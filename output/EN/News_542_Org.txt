China's top legislature concludes standing committee sessionBEIJING -- The Standing Committee of the 13th National People's Congress, China's top legislature, concluded its 23rd session Wednesday in Beijing.
Lawmakers voted to adopt a decision to amend the Copyright Law, and a law on support for veterans.

BEIJING -- Chinese Vice-Premier Hu Chunhua on Tuesday called for strong measures to promote the census and protection of crop germplasm resources to consolidate the development foundation of the seed industry. 
Hu, also a member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee, made the remarks while attending a meeting on the census of crop germplasm resources. 
A thorough census of these resources should be conducted, and a national crop-germplasm resources catalog should be created to protect the resources scientifically, Hu noted. 
He also called for solid work in managing situ conservation areas and building germplasm resources pools.

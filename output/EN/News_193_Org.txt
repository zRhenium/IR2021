BEIJING - Senior Chinese leaders Saturday joined national political advisers in different group discussions at the fourth session of the 13th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. 
The leaders -- Li Keqiang, Li Zhanshu, Wang Yang, Wang Huning, Zhao Leji and Han Zheng -- are all members of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee. 
When joining discussions with political advisers from the economic sector, Premier Li Keqiang called for an accurate understanding of the new development stage, full application of the new development philosophy, and creation of the new development paradigm to promote high-quality development.

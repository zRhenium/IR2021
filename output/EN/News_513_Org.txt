New Party chiefs of Jilin, Hunan, Guizhou, Yunnan appointedBEIJING - Jing Junhai has been appointed as secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), replacing Bayanqolu, according to a decision from the CPC Central Committee published Friday.
Bayanqolu will no longer be a member of the CPC Jilin Provincial Committee or its Standing Committee, said the decision.
Xu Dazhe replaced Du Jiahao as secretary of the CPC Hunan Provincial Committee.
Du will no longer be a member of the CPC Hunan Provincial Committee or its Standing Committee.
Shen Yiqin replaced Sun Zhigang as secretary of the CPC Guizhou Provincial Committee.
Sun will no longer be a member of the CPC Guizhou Provincial Committee or its Standing Committee.
Ruan Chengfa replaced Chen Hao as secretary of the CPC Yunnan Provincial Committee.

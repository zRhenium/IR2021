BEIJING -- A senior Chinese official Thursday called for advancing the Party history learning and education campaign in a solid and effective manner. 
Huang Kunming, a member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee, head of the Publicity Department of the CPC Central Committee and head of the leading group for Party history learning and education campaign, made the remarks at a symposium on studying the guiding principles for the education campaign. 
In carrying out the campaign, Huang urged Party members to focus on delivering services to the people and resolving the difficulties people face.

Former Liaoning province senior political advisor arrested for briberyBEIJING - The Supreme People's Procuratorate (SPP) on Saturday announced its decision to arrest Liu Guoqiang, former vice chairman of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, for suspected bribe-taking.
The National Supervisory Commission has completed the investigation into Liu's case and handed it over to prosecuting agencies, the SPP said.

China's cabinet discusses draft gov't work report, 14th Five-Year PlanBEIJING -- China's State Council on Wednesday convened a plenary meeting to discuss a draft government work report and the draft 14th Five-Year Plan (2021-2025) for National Economic and Social Development and the Long-Range Objectives Through the Year 2035.
The two documents will be deliberated at the top legislature's annual session in March.

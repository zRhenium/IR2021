CNNC's deputy chief economist under probeBEIJING -- Liu Houcheng, deputy chief economist of the China National Nuclear Corporation (CNNC), is being investigated for suspected serious violation of Party disciplinary rules and laws, according to China's top anti-graft body Wednesday.

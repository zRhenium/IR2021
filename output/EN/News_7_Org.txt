Shanghai youths mark May 4th with CPC-themed relayTeenagers gather on Tuesday, May 4, 2021 at the site where the first CPC National Congress was held in Shanghai in 1921 to commemorate the history of the Communist Party of China. [Photo by Gao Erqiang / chinadaily.com.cn] 
  
 
Up to 1,000 Shanghai teenagers gathered on Tuesday at the site where the first CPC National Congress was held in Shanghai in 1921 to commemorate the history of the Communist Party of China on the May 4th Youth Day. 
The gathering started with a short lecture on the history of the Communist Party of China on the early morning of the Youth Day.
